Standard Engineering Article

A standard structure for short technical notes so each article is easy to scan, easy to maintain, and still useful when you come back six months later.

Problem

State the actual issue in plain language. If somebody can read only this section and know what failed, what needed to be built, or what question had to be answered, the article is already useful.

Constraints

  • What physical limits mattered.
  • What budget, time, or tool limits mattered.
  • What tradeoffs were non-negotiable.

Solution

Describe what you did and why that approach won over the alternatives. This is where the engineering judgment belongs, not just the final answer.

What To Watch Next Time

  • What was close to failing.
  • What should be measured earlier.
  • What future-you would want documented.
