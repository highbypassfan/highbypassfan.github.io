Outrigger Layout Study

Studying stance, footprint, and support geometry before committing to a final arrangement.

Objective

Collect the dimensions and assumptions that determine whether the support layout behaves the way it should in the field.

What Matters

  • Stance width and contact points.
  • How level conditions affect setup.
  • What deforms first under load.
